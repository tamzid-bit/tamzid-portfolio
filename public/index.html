03 — About & Teaching

From Yash Raj rooms to my own.

Degree in audio engineering from SAE, Pro Tools certified. I cut my teeth at Yash Raj Studios before opening Space Cat Studio in August 2020 — two rooms where analog warmth meets a fully in-the-box workflow.

The work runs from intimate singer-songwriter mixes to brand films, streaming BGM and live theatre. Whatever the source, the job is the same: get out of the song's way, then make it hit.
